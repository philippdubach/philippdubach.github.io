Quick DCF - Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 9, 2026

Overview

Quick DCF is a Chrome extension that displays stock valuation metrics and provides an interactive DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) calculator. This policy explains what data the extension accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Quick DCF does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension does not use analytics, tracking, cookies (of its own), or any form of telemetry.

Data Accessed

The extension makes requests to Yahoo Finance (query1.finance.yahoo.com, query2.finance.yahoo.com) to retrieve publicly available financial data for the stock ticker you enter. This data is used solely to display valuation metrics and perform DCF calculations within the extension popup.

Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's localStorage to save two items locally on your device:

  • The last searched ticker symbol (so it can be restored when you reopen the popup)
  • Whether you have accepted the disclaimer

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Financial data is sourced from Yahoo Finance's publicly accessible API. Quick DCF has no affiliation with Yahoo or Yahoo Finance. Please refer to Yahoo's Privacy Policy for information about how Yahoo handles data.

Permissions

The extension requests host permissions for *.yahoo.com solely to fetch financial data from Yahoo Finance's API. No other permissions are requested.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
